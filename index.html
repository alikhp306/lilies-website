Our Story

The Designer Behind
Unforgettable Events

My journey began at a local flower shop, where I fell in love with the art of floral design. Over time, I discovered my passion for balloon decorations and realized the magic of blending these two art forms.

Now, I specialize in creating unforgettable and stunning decor for any occasion. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, wedding, or special event, my goal is to bring your vision to life with vibrant balloon creations and elegant floral touches.

From breathtaking balloon arches to custom centerpieces, I'm here to make your event truly extraordinary. Let's make your celebration one to remember!

Booking & Payments

Cancellation & Refund Policy

💳

Deposit to Secure Your Date

A 25% non-refundable deposit is required to confirm and hold your event date. The remaining balance is due upon completion of your event.

✅

Full Refund — Early Cancellation

If you cancel more than 5 business days before your event, we will refund your deposit in full — no questions asked.

⚠️

Deposit Retained — Late Cancellation

Cancellations made within 5 business days of your event date will result in the 25% deposit being retained to cover preparation and reserved materials.

🔄

Rescheduling

Need to move your date? We're happy to reschedule with more than 5 business days' notice at no additional charge, subject to availability.

🌸

We understand that plans can change, and we'll always do our best to work with you. If you have any special circumstances, please reach out to us directly via WhatsApp and we'll find the best solution together.
